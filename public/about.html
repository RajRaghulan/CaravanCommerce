About Us

Why We Created Caravan Commerce.

It all started in March of 2020 when the US and the world went under lockdown due to the Coronavirus pandemic. Co-founders Rahul Sundhar, Raj Raghulan and Aryan Mathur saw huge potential as millions of small businesses had to close due to the pandemic with many lacking an online presence. Cold calling and walking around Danville, California talking to a variety of different businesses they were able to understand the challenges which presented an idea to launch an ecommerce platform and services geared towards local business.

The Bay Area tech startup plans on launching on January 1st which is a big goal however one the company feels they can achieve with determination. After months of relentless work the company is finally looking forward to making its mission of helping small businesses a reality. 2020 has been a rough year for small businesses with many facing tremendous losses because of the shutdown. Caravan’s founders Aryan Mathur and Rahul Sundhar say the mission of the company is “building a one stop shop platform to move small businesses online in this tough climate.” It is clear the company’s mission is helping small businesses and providing a variety of services at an affordable price. The company plans on charging a small monthly fee of just 30 dollars a month for businesses to have access to the site.
